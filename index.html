Who are we?

We provide you the one stop portal for all your educational requirements, whether you're a pupil or master, we make your life and learning easier.


Info

Over the course of last year, millions of students and teachers have adapted to online education in India.

Where do we come in?

With students being outside the classroom, teachers are using online tools for delivering lectures. Vocational education and technical training institutions, which have their curriculum majorly based on practical and hands-on training, have also moved online to deliver classes to train the students. We are here to smoothen the learning process.


Features

This project is specifically made for university students, here are some of the features we provide.

Course details

Whether it's about the details regarding syllabus, books, references etc. we have you covered. You will only find the updated version of everything on our site.

Assignments

Your profile tab will have a section to submit your assignments along with information based on your courses.
